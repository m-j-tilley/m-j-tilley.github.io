About Matthew

Matthew Tilley is a Postdoctoral Researcher in Computational Neuroscience at the University of Chicago. He earned his PhD in Neuroscience from the XYZ University, where he focused on artificial neural networks and their applications in understanding complex brain functions.

Matthew's research interests include developing novel machine learning algorithms to better understand the neural basis of cognition and behavior. He is currently working on a project exploring the role of context-dependent gating in artificial neuronal ensembles.
